About Me

As an engineer with experience in both the semiconductor and construction industries, I have leveraged data analysis to optimize resources, manage projects and uncover problems. I am also able to work collaboratively with cross-functional teams to deliver data-driven insights and recommendations. With these skills, I am excited to help organizations make data-driven decisions and achieve their goals.
